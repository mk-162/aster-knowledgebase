# Pausing and resuming

Pause when you stop mid-ride — a mechanical, a café stop, a nature break — so the
break doesn't count against your moving time.

## How it works

- Tap **Pause**. Your clock and distance stop, and your dot freezes where you are.
- Tap **Resume** to pick up again. Your time and distance continue from where
  they left off.
- Your **moving time** is the total elapsed time minus however long you were
  paused.

## What others see

- You **stay in the event** while paused — pausing is not the same as stopping or
  abandoning. You keep your place on the leaderboard.
- Watchers see your dot as paused rather than moving.
- While you're paused, the app won't fire "off-route" or other movement-based
  alerts about you — it knows you've deliberately stopped.

## Good to know

- Pausing is about **timing**, not safety. If you've had an accident, use **SOS**
  rather than (or as well as) pausing — see
  [SOS and crash detection](../safety-and-offline/sos-and-crash-detection.md).
- In a mass-start race, the race clock is the official measure; pausing affects
  your **moving time** but the event itself keeps running.
