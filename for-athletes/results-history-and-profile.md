# Your results, history and profile

## Event results

After an event, your finish appears on the **leaderboard** and on the event's
**results** page (grouped by category, with times and distances). Your finish
screen also links straight there.

## Free-ride history

Your personal rides are in **My Rides**. Each one shows the full route, distance,
time and average speed, and you can download the raw GPS track (GPX).

## Your public profile

Your profile is your public page on Aster. It shows:

- Your display name, photo, banner, short bio and location.
- Your social links (for example Strava or Instagram).
- Your follower and following counts.
- Summary stats — events completed, total distance, and similar.
- Tabs for your completed events, your public rides, and any posts you've shared.

## What stays private

- Your **email address** and password are never shown.
- **Private free rides** are visible only to you and to anyone you've given the
  share link.
- The GPS points outside a ride's start–finish window are never shown to anyone.

## Control what's public

You decide how visible your athlete profile is, and you can edit your details and
links any time. See [Your profile and handle](../account-and-privacy/your-profile-and-handle.md).
You can also export everything you've stored, or delete your account entirely —
see [Export or delete your data](../account-and-privacy/export-or-delete-your-data.md).
