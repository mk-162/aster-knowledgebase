# Register for an event

Registering links you to an event as a participant, so you can be tracked on the
day and appear on the leaderboard.

## Find and join

1. Open **Discover** (or follow a link to the event) to open its page. You can
   view any public or unlisted event page without signing in.
2. Tap **Join**. If the event is **private**, you'll be asked for the access code
   the organiser gave you.
3. Fill in the short form. Depending on the event this may include your **race
   name** (how you appear on the leaderboard), a **bib number** (if the organiser
   lets you choose one), a **category**, and an optional **team**.
4. Tap **Join Event**. You're now registered.

## What gets downloaded

When you register, the app downloads what it needs so you can ride even with no
signal later:

- The **route** and map data for offline viewing.
- The **start and finish** locations.
- Your participant details (name, bib, category).

Your registration is also saved on your phone. That means once you've joined
**once with signal**, you can later open the app at a signal-less start line and
still start your ride. See [What happens when I lose signal?](../safety-and-offline/offline-and-no-signal.md)

## Before the start

- The event appears on your tracking screen with a live **countdown** as the
  start approaches.
- You'll get reminders as the start nears.
- The **Start** button unlocks within the start window (shortly before the
  scheduled start for races and time trials; earlier for group rides and
  long-distance "grand départ" adventures).
- Want friends to follow you? Use **Share watch link** to send them a link — they
  can watch you live with no account.

## Good to know

- **The organiser is the source of truth.** If the organiser changes the start
  time, cancels, or the event has finished, the app will tell you when you try to
  start — even if your saved copy was slightly out of date.
- **Statuses you might see:** *Registered* (joined, not started), *Started*
  (tracking live), *Finished*, *DNF* (did not finish), *DNS* (did not start).
