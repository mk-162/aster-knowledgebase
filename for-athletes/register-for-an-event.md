# Register for an event

Registering links you to an event as a participant, so you can be tracked on
the day and appear in the live rider list and results.

## Find and join

1. Open **Discover** (or follow a link to the event) to open its page. You can
   view any public or unlisted event page without signing in.
2. Tap **Join**. If the event is **private**, you'll be asked for the
   **Event Code** the organiser gave you. Members-only club events can only be
   joined by members of that club — see
   [clubs](../teams-and-clubs/create-or-join-a-club.md).
3. Fill in the short form. Depending on the event this may include your
   **Display Name** (how you appear to spectators and in results), a
   **Bib Number** (only if the organiser lets riders pick their own), a
   **Category**, and a **team** (chosen from teams you belong to).
4. Tap to confirm. You're now registered.

Places can be limited — the app will tell you if the event, or your chosen
category, is full.

## Ballot events

Some big events allocate places by **ballot**. Instead of joining directly,
you enter the ballot; if you're drawn you'll get an **offer** to accept or
decline in [My registrations](manage-your-registration.md). Until you accept
an offer, your place isn't guaranteed.

## What gets downloaded

When you register, the app downloads what it needs so you can start even with
no signal later:

- The **route** and map data.
- The event's start details and your participant details (name, bib,
  category).

Your registration is also saved on your phone. That means once you've joined
**once with signal**, you can later open the app at a signal-less start line
and still start. See
[What happens when I lose signal?](../safety-and-offline/offline-and-no-signal.md)

## Before the start

- The event appears on your tracking screen. Within the last hour before the
  start you'll see a live **countdown**.
- The **Start** button unlocks within the start window: about **15 minutes**
  before the scheduled start for races, time trials and sportives, about
  **30 minutes** for group rides, and about **2 hours** for long-distance
  "grand départ" adventures. Until then it shows when it opens.
- Want friends to follow you? Tap **Share watch link** to send them the
  event's live page — they can watch you with no account.
- If the organiser has enabled rider privacy for the event, you'll also see a
  **Hide my dot** toggle — see
  [Who can see your location](../account-and-privacy/who-can-see-your-location.md).

## Good to know

- **The organiser is the source of truth.** If the organiser changes the start
  time, cancels, or the event has finished, the app will tell you when you try
  to start — even if your saved copy was slightly out of date.
- **Statuses you might see:** *Registered* (joined, not started), *Started*
  (tracking live), *Paused*, *Finished*, *DNF* (did not finish), *DNS* (did
  not start).
