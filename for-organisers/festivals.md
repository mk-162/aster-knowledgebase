# Festivals (multi-race series)

A **festival** groups several related races under one banner — for example a
weekend with 200 km, 100 km and 50 km distances, or a multi-round series.

## Create a festival and add races

1. Create a festival with a name, optional description and header image.
2. Assign your races to it. A race can be added to a festival when you create it
   or later.
3. The festival gets its own public page listing every race, sorted by distance,
   with each race's start time, type, categories and live registration counts.

You can add and remove races from a festival, and you manage both the festival and
its races as their organiser.

## Share a course across races

If several races share the same course (common at a festival), build a **course**
once — its shape, start/finish lines and feed-stop locations — and apply it to
each race. Edit the course in one place and push the changes to every linked race,
rather than re-uploading a GPX for each. See
[Routes, start and finish lines](routes-start-and-finish.md).

## Good to know

- The festival page only shows races a viewer is allowed to see (private races
  stay hidden unless they manage the festival).
- A race that's already live can't be removed from its festival.
