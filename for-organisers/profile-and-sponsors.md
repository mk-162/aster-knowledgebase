# Your organiser profile and sponsors

## Your public page

Your organiser account has a public page at its handle (`…/orgs/@yourhandle`).
You control:

- Your **name**, **logo** and **banner**.
- A **description**, your **website** and **social links**.
- Your **verified** badge (granted by the Aster team).

The page lists your public and unlisted events, and shows your follower count.

## Followers

Riders and fans can **follow** your organiser account. When you publish a new
public or unlisted event, your followers can be notified — a simple way to bring
your audience back for each event. (Private events don't notify followers, by
design.)

## Sponsors

You can add **sponsors** to your organiser account or to an individual event.
Each sponsor has a name, logo and link, and they appear as a row of logos on your
organiser page and event pages — a clean way to give partners visibility.

## Good to know

- Sponsor links are checked for safety before they're shown.
- Keep your logo and banner up to date — they're the first thing riders and
  spectators see on your events.
