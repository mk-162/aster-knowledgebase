# Export or delete your data

## Export your data

You can download a complete copy of the data Aster holds for you, as a
single file. It includes your profile, your event participations and free rides,
all your GPS points, your messages, follows, notifications and registered
devices. It's the full record — your own copy to keep or take elsewhere.

## Delete your account

You can permanently delete your account. This is **irreversible**:

- Your personal data and GPS history are removed.
- Content you posted is detached from you (your name is removed from it).
- Your sign-in identity is deleted, so you can no longer log in.

To protect your ride data, you can't delete your account **while a ride is in
progress** — finish or stop your ride first. You'll also be asked to confirm
clearly (by typing a confirmation phrase) so it can't happen by accident.

## Good to know

- Export is available whenever you're signed in.
- If you only want to step back rather than delete, you can unfollow accounts,
  mute notifications, or make your rides private instead.
