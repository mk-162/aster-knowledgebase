# Your profile and handle

## Edit your profile

From your settings you can edit:

- **Display name** — how you appear on leaderboards and your profile.
- **Photo and banner**.
- **Bio** and **location**.
- **Social links** — for example Strava, Instagram, or your website.
- Whether you **accept direct messages**.
- Whether your followers are **notified when you start** a race or a free ride.

## Your handle (@username)

Your **handle** is your @username — a short, unique name used to link to your
profile and to **@mention** you in posts and comments. You can claim one if it's
available; some reserved words can't be used.

People, teams, events and organisers can all have handles, which is what makes
`@mentions` work across the app. See
[Key concepts](../getting-started/key-concepts.md).

## What's public vs private

- **Public:** your display name, photo, bio, location, social links, follower and
  following counts, your completed events, your public rides and posts.
- **Private:** your email address and password are never shown. Private rides are
  visible only to you and anyone with the share link.

## Good to know

- Links you add are checked for safety before they're shown.
- Changing your photo cleans up the old one automatically.
- To take your data with you or remove your account entirely, see
  [Export or delete your data](export-or-delete-your-data.md).
