# Account and signing in

## Signing in and staying signed in

Sign in with your email and password. Once you're in, you **stay** in — Watch the
Dot does not log you out automatically, even if the network drops or something
errors mid-ride. The only times your session ends are when **you** sign out, or
if the app detects its saved login is corrupted on your device.

If your login ever needs refreshing, you'll see a small banner inviting you to
sign in again — your ride and data stay safe in the meantime.

## Change your password

While signed in, open your settings and choose to change your password. You'll
confirm your current password first, then set a new one.

Forgotten it? Use **Forgot password** on the sign-in screen to get a reset link
by email (see [Create an account](../getting-started/create-an-account.md)).

## Change your email

While signed in, you can change your account email. For security you'll confirm
your password, and we'll send a confirmation link to the **new** address — the
change takes effect once you click it.

## Sign out

Sign out from your profile. Note that signing out is **blocked while you're
actively tracking a ride**, so you can't accidentally end up logged out mid-event.
Finish or stop your ride first.

## Good to know

- One account covers every role — rider, spectator, organiser, team member.
- Your password is never shown or sent in plain text.
