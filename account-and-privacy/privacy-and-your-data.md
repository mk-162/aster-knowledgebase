# Privacy and your data

## What's held about you

- **Profile:** name, email, photo, bio, location, social links.
- **Rides:** your events and free rides — start and end times, distance,
  elevation, and the GPS points that make up your trail.
- **Activity:** who you follow, your posts, comments and reactions, and your
  notifications.
- **Devices:** the devices registered to receive your push notifications.

## How visibility works

- Watching public and unlisted events needs **no account**, so spectators aren't
  asked for data they don't need.
- Your **email and password** are never shown publicly.
- **Private rides** are visible only to you and anyone you give the share link.
- GPS points outside a ride's start–finish window are never shown to anyone.

## You're in control

- Edit or remove your public details any time — see
  [Your profile and handle](your-profile-and-handle.md).
- Choose what you're notified about — see
  [Preferences and quiet hours](../notifications/preferences-and-quiet-hours.md).
- **Export** everything you've stored, or **delete** your account entirely — see
  [Export or delete your data](export-or-delete-your-data.md).

## Good to know

- You can set **emergency contacts** who are alerted if you raise an SOS — these
  are used only for that purpose. See
  [SOS and crash detection](../safety-and-offline/sos-and-crash-detection.md).
