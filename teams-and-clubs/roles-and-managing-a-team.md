# Roles and managing a team

## The roles

| Role | Can do |
|---|---|
| **Owner** | Everything an admin can, plus transfer ownership. There's one owner per team, and they can't leave without transferring first. |
| **Admin** | Approve or decline join requests, remove members, edit the team profile, post to the team feed. |
| **Member** | Appear on the roster, represent the team in events, see team updates. |

Only the owner can add or remove admins.

## Managing members

From the team's management page, admins can:

- Review and **approve / decline** join requests (for by-request teams).
- **Remove** members.
- Edit the team's **profile** (name, logo, banner, description).
- **Post** to the team feed.

## Transferring ownership

The owner can hand the team to any active member — useful when a club changes
captain. After transferring, the previous owner becomes an ordinary member (or
can leave).

## Entering riders into events

Team managers can help enter their members into an event, rather than each rider
registering individually — handy for clubs fielding a squad.

## Good to know

- Join requests now notify the team's owner and admins, so requests don't sit
  unseen.
- Removing a member doesn't delete their rides or results — it just takes them
  off the roster.
