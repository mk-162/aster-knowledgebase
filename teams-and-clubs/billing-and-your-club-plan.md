# Billing and your club plan

The Club plan is a subscription the club takes out once — individual members
never pay Aster anything. This article covers subscribing, managing the plan,
and what happens if it ends.

## Subscribe your club

1. Open your club's page (you need to be the owner or an admin).
2. In the **Upgrade to Club** card, choose **monthly** or **annual** billing.
3. You're taken to a secure card checkout. Once payment completes, the club
   is on the Club plan straight away — weekly rides, the sponsor slot,
   unlimited members and unlimited follows for members all unlock.

Plans are priced by club size — current prices are on
[astertrack.app/pricing](https://astertrack.app/pricing).

**Good to know:** payments happen on the web. If you're in the Android or
iPhone app, the upgrade card shows you a link to open in a browser — complete
the checkout there and the club upgrades all the same.

## Manage or cancel the plan

From the club page, the billing card shows the plan's status and a **Manage
billing** button. That opens a self-serve billing portal where you can update
the card, switch billing period, download invoices, or cancel.

If a payment fails, the club **keeps its Club plan while the issue is
sorted** — there's a grace period, and nothing turns off mid-month. Fix the
card in **Manage billing** and the plan continues.

## If the plan lapses

Cancelled the plan, or let it expire? Two promises:

1. **The plan runs to the end of what you've paid for.** Cancelling doesn't
   cut the club off early.
2. **Nothing is ever deleted.** Your data is never held hostage.

After the paid period ends, the club returns to the free tier:

| | What happens |
|---|---|
| Existing members | **All stay** — even if there are more than 10. Nobody is removed, ever. |
| New members | Joins pause once the club is over the free 10-member limit. |
| Weekly repeating rides | Stop being scheduled — no new occurrences appear. Already-scheduled rides run as normal. |
| One-off club rides | Still work (free-tier feature). |
| Club board, route library, past rides and results | **All stay readable and usable** for members. |
| Sponsor slot | No longer displayed. |
| Members' follow limit | The [5-favourite limit](the-five-favourite-limit.md) applies again to new follows; existing follows are kept. |

Resubscribe at any time and everything picks up where it left off — the
weekly rides start scheduling again from the same templates.
