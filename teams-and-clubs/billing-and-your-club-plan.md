# Club membership and Aster Plus

**Clubs are free. A club never pays a subscription.** Creating a club is free,
and so is running it — rides, the board, routes, RSVP and the register all work
for nothing. The only thing anyone ever pays for is **Aster Plus**, an
individual membership a rider chooses for themselves.

## How club membership works

- A club's **first three members join free**.
- From the **fourth member onward**, each rider takes out **Aster Plus** to
  join as a full member. It's the rider who chooses Plus — never the club.
- New joiners can start with a short **free Aster Plus demo** to try full
  membership before deciding.
- A club can have at most **500 members** — a scale limit, not a paywall.

Aster Plus unlocks **full club membership**: the roster, the club chat and
each ride's thread, RSVP, and group rides. Someone without Plus (once a club
already has three members) can still **follow and watch** the club and its
public rides for free — they just don't take a full member's seat.

## Get Aster Plus

Aster Plus is an **in-app purchase**, bought in the **App Store** (iPhone) or
**Google Play** (Android):

1. When you go to join a club as its fourth-or-later member, the app offers
   **Aster Plus** — with the option of a **free demo** to try it first.
2. Confirm the monthly membership through the store.
3. You're a full member straight away — roster, chat, RSVP and group rides all
   unlock.

Current prices are on
[astertrack.app/pricing](https://astertrack.app/pricing).

## Manage or cancel Aster Plus

Aster Plus is managed where you bought it — in your **App Store** or **Google
Play** subscriptions. From there you can update payment or cancel. There's
nothing to manage on the club page, because
the club isn't paying for anything.

## If your Aster Plus lapses

Let Plus expire, or cancel it? **Lapsing is safe. Nothing is deleted and no
one is removed.**

- You simply drop back to **following and watching** the club — the same as any
  non-member.
- Your rides, results, posts and history all **stay exactly as they are**.
- **Existing members are never removed.** A club never loses members because
  someone's Plus lapsed.

Take Aster Plus out again whenever you like and you're back to full membership
— everything has been waiting for you.

**Good to know:** because the club never subscribes, there's no club billing to
fail, no grace period to worry about, and no plan that can switch club tools
off. The tools are free for every club, forever.
