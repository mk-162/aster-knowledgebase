# What clubs and teams are on Aster

A **team** on Aster is any group of athletes under one name — a cycling club, a
running club, a group of mates, a race squad. "Club" and "team" are the same
thing in the app; a club is simply a team.

Every team gets a page of its own with a roster, a rides tab, a club board, a
route library, and a record of the events its members have taken part in.
Anyone can create one, free.

## Every club is free — riders choose Aster Plus

There is **no Club plan and no club subscription**. Running a club — its rides,
board, routes, RSVPs and register — is free, always. The club itself never pays
Aster anything.

Membership works simply:

- A club's **first three members join free**.
- From the **fourth member onward**, each rider takes out **Aster Plus** — an
  individual membership, not a club plan — to join as a full member. New
  joiners can try a short **free Aster Plus demo** first.
- A club can have at most **500 members**. That's a scale limit, not a paywall
  — nobody is ever charged to keep the club running.

Aster Plus unlocks **full club membership** — the roster, the club chat and
ride threads, RSVP, and group rides. Without Plus (once a club has three
members) a person can still **follow and watch** the club and its public rides
for free.

| What you get | On every club |
|---|---|
| Team page, roster, follows | Free |
| First three members | Free |
| Fourth member onward | Each rider chooses Aster Plus |
| Members-only group rides (one-off and weekly) | Free to run |
| Invite links | Free |
| Club board (members-only posts) | Free |
| Club route library | Free |
| RSVP list, attendance register and CSV export | Free |
| "Everyone home" view for ride organisers | Free |
| Club sponsor strip on your pages | Available to any club |
| Maximum members | 500 |

Aster Plus is a monthly membership, bought in the App Store or Google Play.
Current prices are on
[astertrack.app/pricing](https://astertrack.app/pricing). See
[club membership and Aster Plus](billing-and-your-club-plan.md) for the detail.

**Good to know:** taking part in events and spectating are free for everyone,
always. Aster Plus is an individual membership — it never gates riding, running
or watching.

## What stays free for everyone

- **Spectating.** Anyone can watch a public event or a shared ride link
  without an account — see [share links](../for-spectators/share-links.md).
- **Taking part.** Registering for events, tracking rides and runs, results —
  all free.
- **Being in a team.** A club's first three members join free; from the fourth
  member on, each rider takes out Aster Plus to join as a full member. The club
  never pays.

## Where to find teams

In the app, open **Groups** (the Clubs area, with tabs for Clubs, Teams and
Riders) to browse and search, or follow a link straight to a club's page. Each
team page shows its logo, description, member roster and tabs for **Rides**,
**Feed** (the club board), **Routes** and **Events**.
