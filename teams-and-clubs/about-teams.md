# About teams

A **team** (or club) groups riders together under one name. Teams are open to
everyone — anyone can create one, join one, or follow one.

## What a team has

- A public **profile page** at its handle (`…/teams/@yourteam`) with a logo,
  banner, description, and a member **roster**.
- **Followers** — people who follow the team for its updates, whether or not they
  are members.
- A **feed** where team admins can post updates that members and followers see.
- A record of the **events** team members have taken part in.

## Teams in events

When you register for an event, you can represent your team. Team managers can
also help enter their riders into events. On leaderboards and results, a rider's
team is shown alongside their name.

## Verified / pro teams

Teams can apply for **pro** status, reviewed by the Aster team. Pro teams
are highlighted in search.
