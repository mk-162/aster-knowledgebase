# Create or join a team

## Create a team

1. Choose to create a team and give it a **name**, **description**, and a
   **logo/banner**.
2. Choose how people join:
   - **Open** — anyone can join instantly.
   - **By request** — people ask to join and an admin approves them.
3. You become the team **owner** and first admin, and the team gets its handle.

## Join a team

- **Open team:** open its page and tap **Join** — you're in straight away.
- **By-request team:** tap **Request to join** (you can add a short message). The
  team's owner and admins review it and approve or decline; you'll be notified
  either way.

## Follow vs join

- **Following** a team means you see its updates in your feed and get its
  notifications — you don't have to be a member.
- **Joining** makes you part of the roster and represents you under the team in
  events.

## Leaving

You can leave a team at any time. The one exception is the **owner**, who must
first hand ownership to another member (so a team is never left without an
owner). See [Roles and managing a team](roles-and-managing-a-team.md).
