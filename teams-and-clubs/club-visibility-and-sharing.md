# Club visibility and sharing

A club page has a public face and a members-only inside. This article maps
out which is which.

## What anyone can see on a club page

- The club's **name, logo, banner and description**.
- The **member roster**.
- The **Events** tab — public events the club's members have taken part in.
- Any **public or unlisted events the club hosts** (a club open day, say) on
  its Rides tab.
- The club's **sponsor strip**, if it has one (Club plan or
  [verified teams](verified-pro-teams.md)).

Anyone can **follow** a club from its page to get its public updates in
their feed.

## What's members-only

- [Club rides](club-rides.md) — members-only rides don't even appear in the
  club page's ride list for non-members, and the ride pages, live map,
  register and results are all members-only. Club rides **never appear in
  Discover** or public event lists.
- [The club board](the-club-board.md) — all posts.
- [The club route library](the-club-route-library.md).

## Sharing a club-ride link

Every ride card and ride page has a share button that copies the ride's
link — the natural way to nudge clubmates: "you in for Saturday?".

- A **member** who opens it lands on the ride, one tap from **I'm in**.
- A **non-member** (or someone signed out) sees a members-only notice with a
  prompt to sign in or ask the club to join. The link never exposes the
  ride's map, riders or route to non-members.

## Sharing an invite link

To bring new people into the club itself (rather than a single ride), admins
share an **invite link** from the club page. Anyone who taps it lands on a
join page showing only the club's name, logo and member count, and joins as a
full member in one tap. The preview reveals nothing members-only. See
[invite links](roles-and-managing-members.md#invite-links).

## The club sponsor slot

Clubs on the [Club plan](billing-and-your-club-plan.md) (and verified teams)
can display a sponsor on the club page and on club-ride pages — a
display-only strip, typically the local bike shop that puts money behind the
club. It's visible to whoever can see the page it's on.
