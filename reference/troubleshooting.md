# Troubleshooting

The issues people hit most, with the quick fix and a link to the full
explanation. If yours isn't here, see
[Getting help and reporting a problem](getting-support.md).

## Watching

**An athlete's dot isn't moving.**
They're almost certainly in an area with no phone signal. Their points are
being saved on their phone and will upload — and the dot, trail and distance
will catch up — when they get signal back. Nothing is lost. (If they've
finished, their dot stays at the line for good.) See
[Reading the live map and trails](../for-spectators/the-live-map-and-trails.md).

**I'm asked to sign in to watch a public event.**
You shouldn't be — public and unlisted events, and shared rides, are open with
no account. Try the link again in a fresh browser tab; if it persists, please
[report it](getting-support.md). (Private and members-only events do require
signing in.)

**A shared link doesn't work.**
The ride may have ended, the athlete may have revoked the link, or the event
may have been switched to private. Ask whoever shared it for an up-to-date
link. See [Share links](../for-spectators/share-links.md).

## Mid-ride problems

**Important:** never sign out or reinstall the app to fix a mid-ride problem.
Your ride data is safe on the phone, and the app recovers by itself.

**"Session needs refreshing" / I can't sign in mid-ride.**
Your ride keeps recording regardless — the app never signs you out over a
network or server error. A banner ("Your ride is saved — sign in to sync")
will sit at the top of the screen; tap it and sign in whenever convenient,
with signal. Your ride and everything recorded stay on the device until then.
See [Account and signing in](../account-and-privacy/account-and-signing-in.md).

**My ride didn't upload.**
It will. Open the app somewhere with signal and it drains the queue
automatically — rides survive app restarts and phone reboots, and late data
fills the ride in up to the moment you stopped. Don't reinstall the app: the
unsent data is stored on the device. See
[What happens when I lose signal?](../safety-and-offline/offline-and-no-signal.md).

**Android keeps killing the tracking in the background.**
Some Android brands (Samsung, Xiaomi, OnePlus, Huawei and others) aggressively
shut down background apps to save battery. Aster expects this: it restarts its
own tracking when you reopen the app, and no ride is ever ended because the
phone interfered. To prevent the interruptions in the first place, exclude
Aster from your phone's battery optimisation ("Unrestricted" battery use) and
don't swipe the app away mid-ride. See
[Tips for a reliable track](../for-athletes/tips-for-a-reliable-track.md).

## Starting and finishing

**GPS is slow to lock at the start.**
Step into the open, away from buildings and tree cover, and give it a moment.
If you need to get going, **Start anyway** appears once the wait has run —
accuracy improves as you move.

**A save failed when I finished.**
Your finish is already recorded — the app captured the moment you tapped, and
a retry keeps that exact time. Tap retry once you have signal.

**I crossed the line but it still shows me racing.**
Automatic finish is detected from your uploaded position, so it can't confirm
while you're offline. Once you're back in signal your queued positions upload
and the finish is confirmed at the moment you actually crossed. If it still
doesn't settle, the organiser can finish you from their dashboard. See
[Finishing a ride](../for-athletes/finishing-a-ride.md).

## Follows and clubs

**"Follow limit reached."**
Free accounts can follow up to 5 athletes as favourites. Membership of a
subscribed club removes the cap; unfollowing someone frees a slot. The cap
never affects watching — a shared link or public event page works for anyone.
See [Teams and clubs](../teams-and-clubs/index.md).

**Our weekly club ride hasn't appeared.**
Upcoming recurring rides are created from the club's template while the club's
plan is active. If the plan has lapsed, new rides stop being created — but
nothing is deleted, and past rides stay readable. Once the plan is renewed,
the recurring rides resume. See [Teams and clubs](../teams-and-clubs/index.md).

## Notifications

**I'm not getting push notifications.**
Check your device's system notification settings for Aster, make sure the
master push switch is on and the type isn't muted, and confirm you're not in
your quiet-hours window. Reopening the app refreshes your device registration.
See [Push notifications and devices](../notifications/push-and-devices.md).

## App updates

**The app shows "Update available" or reloads itself.**
That's the app refreshing to the latest version after a release. Let it
reload; if prompted, tap to load the latest version.

## Still stuck?

See [Getting help and reporting a problem](getting-support.md) — for event
questions ask the organiser; for everything else, email the Aster team.
