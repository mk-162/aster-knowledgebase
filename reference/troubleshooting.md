# Troubleshooting

## Tracking

**The Start button is greyed out.**
The start window may not be open yet, or the event isn't ready to start. Check the
countdown on the event.

**GPS is slow to lock at the start.**
Step into the open, away from buildings and tree cover, and let the warm-up run.
Tap **Start anyway** once accuracy looks good.

**My dot isn't moving for watchers.**
You're probably in a no-signal area. Your points are being saved and will upload —
and your dot will catch up — when you get signal. Nothing is lost.

**A save failed when I finished.**
If you're offline you'll see a retry prompt; your finish time is already saved on
the phone. Tap retry once you have signal.

**I crossed the line but it still shows me racing.**
Automatic finish can take a moment, and can't confirm while you're offline. You
can tap **Finish** yourself, or ask the organiser to finish you from their
dashboard. See [Finishing a ride](../for-athletes/finishing-a-ride.md).

## Notifications

**I'm not getting push notifications.**
Check your device's system notification settings for Aster, make sure the
master push switch is on and the type isn't muted, and confirm you're not in your
quiet-hours window. Reopening the app refreshes your device registration. See
[Push notifications and devices](../notifications/push-and-devices.md).

## Watching

**I'm asked to sign in to watch a public event.**
You shouldn't be — public and unlisted events are open. Try the link again or in a
fresh browser tab. (Private events do require signing in.)

**A shared link doesn't work.**
The event may have been switched to private, or a free-ride link may have expired.
Ask whoever shared it for an up-to-date link.

## App updates

**The app shows "Update available" or reloads itself.**
That's the app refreshing to the latest version after a release. Let it reload; if
prompted, tap to load the latest version.

## Still stuck?

If something isn't covered here, contact the Aster team or your event's
organiser.
