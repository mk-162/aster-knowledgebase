# Frequently asked questions

## Watching

**Do I need an account to watch a race?**
No — public and unlisted events, and any ride shared with you by link, are
watchable with no account. Private events require signing in. See
[Watch an event live](../for-spectators/watch-an-event-live.md).

**How do I find an event to watch?**
Open the link the organiser or rider shared, or browse **Discover** for public
events. Unlisted and private events aren't listed — you need the link (and, for
private, the access code).

**Why does a rider's trail have gaps?**
The rider lost phone signal there, so their points couldn't upload until they
reconnected. The gap fills in when they catch up. See
[Reading the live map and trails](../for-spectators/the-live-map-and-trails.md).

**Why has a rider's dot stopped?**
Either they've paused, they're in a no-signal area, or they've finished. A
finished rider's dot stops at the line for good.

## Riding

**Can I start a ride with no phone signal?**
Yes. Starting and finishing both work fully offline; your points are saved on the
phone and upload when signal returns. See
[What happens when I lose signal?](../safety-and-offline/offline-and-no-signal.md).

**Will the app drain my battery?**
You choose a battery mode to match your ride length, from Live to Ultra. See
[Battery modes](../for-athletes/battery-modes.md).

**Will the app ever stop my ride or log me out by itself?**
No. Your ride continues until you finish or abandon it, you cross the line, or the
organiser's cut-off passes — and you're never logged out mid-ride.

**Why does my race time not start at zero when I tapped Start?**
In a mass-start race, everyone is timed from the **gun**, not from the moment they
individually tapped Start. See
[Starting and tracking a ride](../for-athletes/starting-and-tracking-a-ride.md).

**Do I need to keep the screen on or the app open?**
No. Leave the app running in the background with the screen off — it keeps
recording. Just don't force-quit (swipe away) the app mid-ride. See
[Tips for a reliable track](../for-athletes/tips-for-a-reliable-track.md).

**I forgot to stop my free ride and it tracked my drive home.**
Stop it as soon as you notice. For events this isn't an issue — finishing freezes
your ride at the line. For free rides, stopping ends the ride; get into the habit
of tapping **Stop & save** when you're done.

**Can I change my category or withdraw after registering?**
Usually yes, while registration is open — see
[Manage or cancel your registration](../for-athletes/manage-your-registration.md).
What's allowed, and until when, is set by the organiser.

## Privacy & safety

**Who can see my location?**
Only while you're tracking, and only the right people: in an event, whoever can
view that event (per its privacy level); on a free ride, just the people you send
the link to. Full detail in
[Who can see your location?](../account-and-privacy/who-can-see-your-location.md).

**Is the app tracking me when I'm not riding?**
No. Your location is only recorded between the moment you start a ride and the
moment you finish.

**How do I keep a ride private?**
Use a free ride (private by default) and don't share the watch link, and check an
event's privacy level before joining. See
[Who can see your location?](../account-and-privacy/who-can-see-your-location.md).

**Does SOS call the emergency services?**
No — it's an in-app alert and live location share to your watchers, organiser and
[emergency contacts](../safety-and-offline/emergency-contacts.md). Always carry a
way to call for real help.

## Data, battery & devices

**Will tracking use a lot of mobile data?**
No. The main download is the route/map (do it on Wi-Fi beforehand); tracking
itself is light, and with no signal it uses no data at all. Watching others live
uses more. See [Mobile data, battery and what runs offline](../for-athletes/data-usage.md).

**Will it drain my battery?**
You pick a battery mode for the ride length, and it can auto-drop to a more
economical mode when low. See [Battery modes](../for-athletes/battery-modes.md).

**My battery died mid-ride — did I lose my ride?**
No. Everything recorded is saved on the phone. Charge up, reopen the app, and it
uploads what's queued.

**Does it work with my Apple Watch, bike computer or Garmin?**
Tracking is done by the **phone app** — there's no separate watch or
bike-computer tracking today. You can add your Strava (and other) links to your
profile so people can find you there.

## Results & accuracy

**Why does the leaderboard match my finish screen exactly?**
Every screen reads from the same trusted set of GPS points, so the numbers agree.
See [Why your distance and time are accurate](../safety-and-offline/data-accuracy.md).

**My ride saved short / a section is missing.**
If you were offline, the missing part usually fills in once your phone uploads its
saved points. Open the app with signal to let it catch up.

## Account

**How do I reset my password?**
Use **Forgot password** on the sign-in screen. See
[Account and signing in](../account-and-privacy/account-and-signing-in.md).

**How do I delete my account or download my data?**
Both are in your settings. See
[Export or delete your data](../account-and-privacy/export-or-delete-your-data.md).
