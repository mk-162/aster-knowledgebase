# Getting help and reporting a problem

Stuck? Here's the fastest route to an answer.

## First, check these

Most questions are answered already:

- **[Troubleshooting](troubleshooting.md)** — fixes for the common issues
  (GPS lock, push notifications, "can't see the event", app updates).
- **[Frequently asked questions](faq.md)** — the questions people ask most.
- Your section's guides: [athletes](../for-athletes/), [spectators](../for-spectators/),
  [organisers](../for-organisers/).

## Who to ask

- **Questions about a specific event** — registration, your category, start times,
  results — are best answered by **that event's organiser**. They control the
  event's settings.
- **App problems, account help, or anything platform-wide** — email the **Aster team** at
  [help@astertrack.app](mailto:help@astertrack.app) — a human reads every message.

## Reporting a bug well

A good report gets fixed faster. Please include:

- **What you did**, what you **expected**, and what **happened** instead.
- **Which event or ride** it was about (a link helps).
- Your **device and app version** (for example "iPhone 14, app 2.3").
- The **time** it happened (and your time zone), so it can be traced.
- A **screenshot** if you can.

## If it's an emergency on a ride

Aster's **SOS** raises an in-app alert and shares your location with your
watchers, organiser and emergency contacts — but it is **not** a call to the
emergency services. In a real emergency, call for help directly. See
[SOS and crash detection](../safety-and-offline/sos-and-crash-detection.md).
