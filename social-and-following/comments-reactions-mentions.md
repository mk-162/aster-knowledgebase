# Comments, reactions and mentions

## Comments

You can comment on stories and event-feed posts, and reply to other comments in a
thread. Comments can include an image or GIF. Whoever you reply to, and the post's
author, get a notification.

## Reactions

React to a story with one of a set of emoji (a heart, a celebrate, and a few
cycling-flavoured ones like "fast" and "grit"). The author gets a notification
when you react.

## Mentions

Type **@** followed by a handle to mention someone — a rider, a team, an event or
an organiser — in a post, story or comment. The person or team you mention gets a
**mention** notification and the @name links to their page. See
[Key concepts: handles](../getting-started/key-concepts.md).

## Good to know

- You can see and engage with anything that's **public**; followers-only content
  is visible if you follow the author.
- If a comment or post is removed, its text is cleared but the thread stays
  readable.
- Manage which of these generate notifications for you in
  [Preferences and quiet hours](../notifications/preferences-and-quiet-hours.md).
