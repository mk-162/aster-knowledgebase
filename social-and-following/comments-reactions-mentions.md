# Comments, reactions and mentions

## Comments

You can comment on posts, stories and event-feed posts, and reply to other
comments in a thread. A comment can be text, or a **GIF or image on its own** —
a GIF with no words counts as a comment. When you reply to someone, and the
post's author, both get a notification.

**Deleting a comment:** you can delete your own comments. The post's author (or,
on an event feed, the event's organiser) can also remove comments on their post.

## Reactions

React to a post or story with one of six emoji:

- ❤️ Love
- 🎉 Celebrate
- 💨 Fast
- 💪 Strength
- 🥶 Grit
- 🤯 Wow

The author is notified when you react. Tap your reaction again to remove it.

## Mentions

Type **@** and start a name to mention someone — an athlete, a team, an event or
an organiser. Pick them from the list, and the **@name links to their page**.

When you mention a **person**, they get a mention notification. Mentioning a team,
event or organiser links to its page but doesn't send it a notification.

See [key concepts: handles](../getting-started/key-concepts.md) for how @names
work.

## Good to know

- You can see and engage with anything **public**; followers-only content is
  visible only if you follow the author.
- If a comment or post is removed, its text is cleared but the thread stays
  readable.
- Choose which of these buzz your phone in
  [Preferences and quiet hours](../notifications/preferences-and-quiet-hours.md).
