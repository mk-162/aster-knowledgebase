# Following and your feed

## Who you can follow

- **Riders** — keep up with their rides and posts.
- **Teams** — see team updates and events.
- **Events** — get notified about the event and the riders in it.
- **Organisers** — be notified when they publish new events.

Tap **Follow** on any profile or event page. Following is public — your followers
and the people you follow are shown on your profile. Tap **Unfollow** any time.

## Your feed

Your feed shows the latest stories from you and the people you follow (respecting
each story's visibility). It's the easiest way to keep up with everyone's riding.

## Live now

When riders you follow are **out riding right now** (and have chosen to share with
followers), they appear in a **live now** strip — tap straight through to watch
them live.

## Getting notified

Following is what powers most notifications. Choose exactly which alerts you get
in [Notification types](../notifications/notification-types.md) and
[Preferences and quiet hours](../notifications/preferences-and-quiet-hours.md).

## Good to know

- New riders are auto-followed to a starter account so their feed isn't empty on
  day one — you can unfollow anyone you like.
- Following an **event** is separate from following the **riders** in it; do both
  if you want the full picture.
