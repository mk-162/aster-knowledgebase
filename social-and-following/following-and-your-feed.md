# Following and your feed

## Who you can follow

- **Athletes** — keep up with their rides, runs and posts.
- **Teams and clubs** — see their updates and events.
- **Events** — get updates about the event and the riders in it.
- **Organisers** — hear when they publish a new event.

Tap **Follow** on any profile or event page. Following is public — the people you
follow and your own followers are shown on your profile. Tap **Unfollow** any
time.

Following is **free and unlimited** — follow as many people, teams and events as
you like, and unfollow any time. Watching a ride isn't following: you can always
open a share link or watch a public event, on any device, with no account.

## Finding people: Discover

**Discover** (the search button in the app) is where you find who and what to
follow. It has tabs for **Events**, **Festivals**, **Organisers**, **Teams** and
**Riders** — leave the search empty to browse popular and active ones, or type to
search a tab.

## Your feed

Your home feed shows the latest posts and stories from you and the people you
follow, respecting each post's visibility. It's the simplest way to keep up with
everyone's riding and running.

## Live now

When athletes you follow are **out on a ride or run right now** — and have chosen
to let followers watch — they appear in a **live now** strip. Tap straight through
to watch them live. Whether your own live rides and races show up here is your
call, set under **Who can watch me** in
[Notification settings](../notifications/preferences-and-quiet-hours.md).

## Getting notified

Following is what powers most notifications. Choose exactly which alerts you get
in [Notification types](../notifications/notification-types.md) and
[Preferences and quiet hours](../notifications/preferences-and-quiet-hours.md).

## Good to know

- New accounts start out already following the Aster founder, so your feed isn't
  empty on day one. Unfollow anyone you like.
- Following an **event** is separate from following the **riders** in it — do both
  for the full picture.
