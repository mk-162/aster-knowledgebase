# Notification types

Aster can notify you about the riders, teams and events you follow, and
about activity on your own account. You control which of these you receive — see
[Preferences and quiet hours](preferences-and-quiet-hours.md).

## Following riders and races

- **Athlete started** — someone you follow has signed up for / started a race.
- **Finish** — a rider you're watching has finished (or didn't finish).
- **Progress updates** — milestones for a rider you're watching, such as reaching
  a round distance, completing a lap, or passing a checkpoint.
- **Gap change** — the time gap between your rider and the leader changed by more
  than the amount you choose.
- **Stopped** — a rider you're watching has been stationary for a while.
- **Off route** — a rider you're watching has left the course.

## Safety

- **SOS** — a rider has raised an emergency alert (or crash detection did). These
  always come through, even during quiet hours.

## Social

- **New follower** — someone followed you.
- **Mention** — someone @mentioned you in a post, story or comment.
- **Comment** — someone commented on your post or story.
- **Reaction** — someone reacted to your story.
- **Message** — you received a direct message.

## Events you follow

- **Event reminders** — the countdown to a race you've registered for
  (a day before, a couple of hours before, and so on).
- **New event** — an organiser, team or rider you follow has published a new event.
- **Organiser update** — an organiser posted to the feed of an event you're in or
  following.

## Teams

- **Team updates** — your request to join a team was received, approved or
  declined, or you were made the owner of a team.

## Where notifications appear

- **Push notifications** on your phone (via the app).
- **In-app** in your notification list (the bell), so you can catch up on anything
  you missed even if push was off.

See [Push notifications and devices](push-and-devices.md) and
