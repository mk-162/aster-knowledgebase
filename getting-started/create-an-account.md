# Create an account

## Do I even need one?

Not always. **Watching** public and unlisted events — and any ride someone
shares with you by link — works with no account at all.

You need an account when you want to **do** something that's tied to you:

- Register for an event and be tracked as an athlete.
- Record your own free rides.
- Follow athletes, teams or events and get notifications.
- Post updates, comment, or react.
- Join a team or club.
- Create or manage an event as an organiser.

## Sign up

You can sign up in the app or on the website — it's the same account either way.

1. Choose **Sign Up**.
2. Enter your **display name** (the name shown on results and your profile),
   your **email**, and a **password** of at least 8 characters.
3. Enter your **date of birth**. If you're under 16, a parent or guardian must
   tick the consent box for you.
4. Accept the **privacy policy** (required) — the marketing tick-box is
   optional.
5. Confirm — your account is created and you're signed in.

You'll receive a short series of welcome emails over your first week
introducing the main things you can do.

Aster signs you in with your email and password only — there is no
sign-in-with-Google/Apple and no email-link sign-in.

## Sign in

Enter your email and password. Once you're signed in, you **stay** signed in —
the app does not log you out automatically, even if the network drops or an
error occurs mid-ride. This is deliberate: being logged out in the middle of
nowhere would be a real problem. See
[What happens when I lose signal?](../safety-and-offline/offline-and-no-signal.md)

The only times your session ends are when **you** tap Sign Out, or if the app
detects its saved login is corrupted on your device. If your session ever needs
refreshing mid-ride, a banner asks you to sign in again — your ride keeps
recording and nothing on your device is lost in the meantime.

## Forgotten password

On the sign-in screen choose **Forgot password?**, enter your email, and we'll
send a reset link. Open it and choose a new password (at least 8 characters).
For security we always say the same thing whether or not the address is
registered — so you won't be told whether an email exists in the system.

For changing your password or email **while signed in**, see
[Account and signing in](../account-and-privacy/account-and-signing-in.md).

## Good to know

- **One account, all roles.** The same account works as an athlete, a
  spectator, an organiser and a team member. You don't need separate logins.
- **The app keeps your data on your device.** Your login and any ride in
  progress are stored on the phone so the app works offline. They stay there
  until you explicitly sign out.
- **You can delete your account and export your data** at any time from
  **Settings → Account**. See
  [Export or delete your data](../account-and-privacy/export-or-delete-your-data.md).
